Welcome to Our Page!

Fun and engaging education platform for your little stars ✨

Why is the website’s name Unplugged, you might ask.

The word unplugged usually means "not connected to electricity" or "without being plugged in." It can also mean taking a break from constant screen time and digital distractions.

In our case, Unplugged symbolizes the idea of using technology more intentionally — not just for mindless entertainment, but for learning and discovery.

With our Guess the Animal Sound game, we want children to engage with their devices not just for entertainment, but also for learning. By playing this, they can improve their listening skills and discover the world of animals, turning screen time into a fun, educational experience, also helping them "unplug" from passive content and plug into learning.
